- Nicolas Oury and Wouter Swierstra. 2008.

  ACM, New York, NY, USA, 39-50.
  [[ACM](https://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=1411213)]
  [[PDF](https://cs.ru.nl/~wouters/Publications/ThePowerOfPi.pdf)]
